## What this is

A free interactive visualizer for MIPS datapath and Karnaugh maps. Use at your own discretion.

## Use it for

- Learning low-level computer architecture
- Boolean function minimization practice
- Sharing sim states with peers
- Anything pedagogically useful

## Don't use it for

- Sharing abusive content via shared permalinks
- Automated scraping of the entire shared-snapshot space
- Reverse-engineering the underlying infrastructure to attack other users
- Anything else that would obviously be jerk-shaped

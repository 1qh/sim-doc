## What this is

A free interactive visualizer for MIPS datapath and Karnaugh maps. It runs entirely in your browser. Use at your own discretion.

## Use it for

- Learning low-level computer architecture
- Boolean function minimization practice
- Sharing sim states with peers via a link
- Anything pedagogically useful

## Don't use it for

- Sharing abusive content via shared links
- Reverse-engineering the site to attack other users
- Anything else that would obviously be jerk-shaped

## Substrate licensing

The substrate packages (everything under `packages/*` in the public OSS repo) are MIT-licensed. You can use them in your own projects per MIT terms.

The product application (this site itself) is proprietary. The substrate is what's open.

## Anonymous use

There are no accounts and no sign-in. Everyone uses the tool anonymously. Your work stays in your own browser; sharing happens by copying a link that carries the state.

## Availability

This is a free tool. We don't guarantee uptime, data retention, or performance. Your saved snapshots live in your own browser, so their retention is up to your device and browser. We try our best (per the operational docs in this repo) but no SLA.
